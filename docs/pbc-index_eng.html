PBC Commodity Index

Центр ценовых индексов (ЦЦИ) – российское ценовое агентство. Оперативно публикуем цены на товары российского экспорта и логистику.

Switch to Russian

PBC Commodity Index - а composite price index that measures price changes of Russian commodity export comparing to the level of January 2022.

PBC Commodity Index change to the previous week: +1.2 p. (+27.7 p. over the previous four weeks)

The biggest commodity price changes over the week: Crude Oil: +1.1%, Fertilizers Products: +0.5%

Price differential (discount) - the difference in price dynamics in Russian main export commodities compared to the prices of world benchmarks.
